# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant 2.1. The full text is at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.

In short: be respectful, focus on the work, give and accept feedback graciously, and assume good intent. Personal attacks, harassment, and disruptive behavior have no place here.

To report a concern, contact the maintainer at the email address listed in the site footer. Reports are kept confidential.
